Top 3 places to visit in Vietnam

Saigon

Saigon (TP Ho Chi Minh) is a lively city with busy markets, cafes, and a mix of old and new architecture

Da Nang

Da Nang is a coastal city known for its sandy beaches, the Marble Mountains, and the iconic Golden Bridge

TP Hue

Hue is a historic city with ancient citadels, royal tombs, and pagodas along the Perfume River
